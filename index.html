A corporate-backed initiative to introduce automated, premium helmet sanitization to Visakhapatnam's most prominent fuel station.

The Problem

Visakhapatnam's coastal humidity forces two-wheeler riders to constantly deal with sweat-soaked helmets. This leads to severe bacteria buildup, unpleasant odors, and hair damage.

Despite being mandatory safety gear, helmets are rarely cleaned because there is no fast, on-the-go solution available in the city. Riders simply endure the discomfort daily.

The Solution

Our automated helmet sanitizing station transforms a sweaty nuisance into a fresh, hygienic experience in just a few minutes.

Using advanced rapid-sanitization mechanisms, the machine neutralizes deep-seated odors and eliminates 99% of bacteria. Placing this unit at the Siripuram bunk provides an instant premium amenity that drivers will actively seek out.
